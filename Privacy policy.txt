Privacy Policy for StreamBase

At StreamBase, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and verify it with an OTP (One-Time Password).
Video Data: The videos you upload are stored in your YouTube account, and we only have access to your YouTube playlist associated with your email ID.
Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the platform to improve your experience.
2. How We Use Your Information

To authenticate your account and ensure secure access.
To store and manage videos in your YouTube account.
To communicate with you regarding your account, updates, or support.
3. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only used for providing our services and improving the platform.

5. Third-Party Services

StreamBase utilizes YouTube API for storing and managing videos. Please review YouTube's privacy policy for further information on their data handling practices.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access, update, or delete your account and personal information.
Request a copy of your data.
Contact us to inquire about the use of your information.
7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the platform.

8. Contact Us
